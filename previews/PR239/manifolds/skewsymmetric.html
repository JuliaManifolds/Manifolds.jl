Skew-symmetric matrices

The Manifold $ \operatorname{SkewSym}(n)$ consisting of the real- or complex-valued skew-symmetric matrices of size $n √ó n$, i.e. the set

\[\operatorname{SkewSym}(n) = \bigl\{p ‚àà ùîΩ^{n √ó n}\ \big|\ p^{\mathrm{H}} = -p \bigr\},\]

where $\cdot^{\mathrm{H}}$ denotes the Hermitian, i.e. complex conjugate transpose, and the field $ùîΩ ‚àà \{ ‚Ñù, ‚ÑÇ\}$.

Though it is slightly redundant, usually the matrices are stored as $n √ó n$ arrays.

Note that in this representation, the (real-valued part of) the diagonal has to be zero, which is also reflected in the manifold_dimension.
