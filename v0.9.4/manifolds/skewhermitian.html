Skew-hermitian matrices

The AbstractManifold $ \operatorname{SkewHerm}(n)$ consisting of the real- or complex-valued skew-hermitian matrices of size $n √ó n$, i.e. the set

\[\operatorname{SkewHerm}(n) = \bigl\{p ‚àà ùîΩ^{n √ó n}\ \big|\ p^{\mathrm{H}} = -p \bigr\},\]

where $\cdot^{\mathrm{H}}$ denotes the Hermitian, i.e. complex conjugate transpose, and the field $ùîΩ ‚àà \{ ‚Ñù, ‚ÑÇ, ‚Ñç\}$.

Though it is slightly redundant, usually the matrices are stored as $n √ó n$ arrays.

Note that in this representation, the real-valued part of the diagonal must be zero, which is also reflected in the manifold_dimension.
